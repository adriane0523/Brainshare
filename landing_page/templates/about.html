Project Narrative

Project Narrative Project Aims Modified Policy Delphi Importance

This project has direct relevance to and will maximize the value of the NIH-funded BRAIN Initiative by proactively engaging diverse stakeholders in anticipating and resolving challenges to sharing human data from BRAIN Initiative studies. This project will provide critical empirical data about specific data sharing challenges and concerns, and will employ a rigorous process of deliberation involving multiple diverse stakeholder groups to generate a comprehensive set of empirically informed policy and practice options that address challenges distinctive to BRAIN Initiative research.
